Joykis Recreational Park - Website Plan

1. Site Name

Joykis Recreational Park - The name reflects a fun-filled adventure and leisure experience where visitors can enjoy a variety of outdoor activities.

2. Site Purpose

The website serves as an information and booking platform for Joykis Recreational Park. It provides details about attractions, adventure activities, pricing, visitor guides, and an online booking system.

3. Target Audience & Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A family looking for a weekend getaway with fun activities for kids.
  • Scenario 2: A group of friends searching for adventure sports such as zip-lining and rock climbing.

4. Color Scheme

  • (Blue): Used for headings, buttons, and borders to give a nature-friendly feel.
  • (Alice Blue): Used as the background color for a fresh and clean look.

5. Typography

The primary font for the website will be Arial, sans-serif, ensuring readability and a modern appearance. This font will be used for body text and headings.
